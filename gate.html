GATE

Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering(GATE) is an exam on the comprehensive understanding of the candidates in various undergraduate subjects in Engineering/Technology/Architecture and post-graduate level subjects in Science. The GATE exam is generally held in the 1-2 weeks of February on Saturday and Sundays.
